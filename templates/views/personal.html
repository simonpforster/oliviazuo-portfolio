Rerendered Memory
2024
Editorial Design
Type Design
Video Production
Full Video
Online Showcase
The rising trend of ‘tech-nostalgia’ reflects a fascination with using old technologies and faux-vintage aesthetics in modern media.
By investigating the iterative process of digital images in screen devices, Rerendered Memory explores the transition from ‘technologies of memory’ to ‘memory of technologies’ within the context of the reminiscence of past media technologies in contemporary memory practices.

Invisible Feast
2023
Editorial Design
3D Modelling
Branding
UI/UX
Invisible Feast discusses the diversity and possibilities of future dietary structures in the current context of the food crisis. By simulating a future in which extinct animals and sustainable protein sources are used as ingredients, efforts towards a “future-proof” diet are re-examined from an ironic perspective.

The Visitor
2022
Editorial Design
Type Design
3D Modelling
The Visitor explores the hypothesis of a future where humans are able to travel back in time to change their regretful decisions as "visitors". It discusses my concerns about human’s self-destructive tendencies: any attempt to change the past for self-protection will only lead to a more collapsed future.

Another Romance
2022
Editorial Design
Video Production
3D Modelling
Another Romance delves into the core of intimate relationships, examining both the overt and subtle bonds—such as emotions, shared spaces, and more—that intertwine individuals. It envisions these connections as a delicate "cocoon," where gestures, emotions, and unspoken messages gradually evolve into the cornerstone of their communication, ultimately giving rise to a unique language that remains impenetrable to outsiders.
